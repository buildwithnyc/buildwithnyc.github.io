Data Products Management Fellow

The Product Team centers the needs of New Yorkers, and works alongside city agencies and partners to use technology to improve service delivery. Our work includes:

  • Developing and maintaining products to improve the lives of low-income New Yorkers by connecting them to clear actionable information on benefits, programs, services, and employment that can help them attain economic opportunity.
  • Make the City’s equity, poverty and workforce data more transparent and actionable so that policy makers can use it to make informed decisions, researchers have creditable data, and the public can better understand it.
  • Design and pilot new tools to improve social service delivery to accelerate the research, design, build, implementation, and evaluation of digital tools and services for New Yorkers.
  • Support open source and scalable technologies so the City and other civic technologists can build upon what works and accelerate their ability to serve New Yorkers.

The team also helps inspire and shape how the City of New York uses modern, agile and user-centric approaches to build technology products.

The Data Products Management Fellow will focus on supporting the office’s data products, which includes EquityNYC, the Workforce Data Portal,and an in-development tool to visualize the City’s poverty data.

EquityNYC is a tool that describes the state of racial and social equity in New York City, and the City’s work to address disparities.

The Workforce Data Portal features data and deep dives on the City’s workforce development system, pulling together core metrics on program participation and outcomes across agencies.

The poverty tool will allow users to interact with the NYCgov poverty measure to show how poverty impacts different groups and how benefits programs and expenses affect poverty.

  Responsibilities

The Product Management Fellow will work alongside the team to:

  • Support communications with stakeholders and agency partners.
  • Support the Data Scientists in cleaning, analyzing, and uploading data.
  • Support Product Manager and Data Scientists in developing data strategy.
  • Develop written materials to communicate data findings and highlight current projects and initiatives.
  • Work with the Product Manager, UX Designers, Content Strategist and Engineers to plan and facilitate sprints.
  • Plan and perform site / application quality assurance.
  • Review and organize user feedback, turning it into actionable items for the team to work on.
  • Prepare analytics reports using quantitative and qualitative data.
  • Produce and maintain product documentation: backlog, product specs, tickets, wikis, etc.
